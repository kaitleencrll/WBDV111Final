About Us

Mission & Vision +

Our heartbeat is establishing a stray-free and compassionate Valenzuela City! 🐾 We are leveraging cutting-edge community-driven digital solutions and high-speed response networks to ensure every animal is seen, heard, and cared for. By connecting local residents with real-time reporting tools, we aim to bridge the gap between stray sightings and professional rescue, fostering a safer environment for both paws and people. 🏛️✨

Project Background +

Born as a research-led initiative by dedicated OLFU students, this project is designed to test and prove the efficiency of mobile-first reporting in critical animal welfare scenarios. 🎓💻 We analyzed the delays in traditional rescue methods and built Furiend as a modern answer to an age-old problem. This is where academic research meets real-world community service, proving that a single report can truly save a life. 🐶📋

System Overview +

Furiend is a fully integrated web ecosystem designed for total transparency and speed. 🌐🚀 Our platform features real-time geolocation tagging to pin-point rescues, automated donation tracking to ensure every cent helps a furry friend, and a robust shelter management module for our partners. It’s a digital sanctuary where logistics meet love, making pet adoption and rescue management as seamless as possible for everyone involved. 🦴💳
